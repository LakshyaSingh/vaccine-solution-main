description: Cold chain monitoring
--- 

The vaccine lots need to be kept as a constant temperature of a time period. The sensors telemetries coming from the refrigerator containers are processed to assess cold chain violation.

For that purpose the solution includes streaming of telemetry events, a stateful microservice to implement aggregate, and alarm generation, and integration to refrigerator service to log issue on the device.
